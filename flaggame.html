Welcome to the Flag Game!

Flag Game is a challenging game of knowledge and nerve where the goal is to build the longest winning streak possible. You are presented with a random flag from around the world and must correctly guess the country it belongs to. Each correct answer, which must be spelled perfectly, increases your score by one. However, the true test lies in the game's unforgiving nature: a single wrong guess or even a minor typo instantly wipes your score back to zero, forcing you to begin your streak again from scratch.

This "all or nothing" scoring system turns a simple quiz into a thrilling test of focus, where the pressure mounts with every correct answer.
